Vision

The Cyber Security Department aims to be distinguished in the field of information security and graduate socially responsible information security professionals and has the expertise, knowledge, and skills that qualify them for local competition.

Mission

The cybersecurity program provides distinctive cybersecurity specialists according to the labor market requirements, contributes to the protection of systems in the national information society.

Program Goals

  • Deliver a high-quality teaching and learning process to develop the ability to produce modern cybersecurity solutions.
  • Develop strong communication skills and adhere to legal and ethical standards..
  • Create a suitable research environment for students and faculty to keep them updated with the most recent cybersecurity technologies.
  • Provide positive cybersecurity awareness to the community.
